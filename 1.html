REDSTONE ESSENTIALS

AN INTRODUCTION TO THE ELECTRIFYING WORLD OF REDSTONE!

Enchantment Table and Redstone Block

Enchantment Table:

Can be used to enchant tools, weaponds and armour in the safety of your own home.

Redstone Block Recipe:

A redstone block can be crafted from 9 pieces of redstone dust.

Did You Know?

A block of redstone can be used as a power source.

Enchanting Your Tools

Are You Ready To Get Fully Wired Up To Redstone?

When mine with an echanted pickaxe, a block of redstone ore will drop up to 8 pieces of redstone dust. You can enchant your tools on an enchantment table. To craft a table you'll need 4 blocks of obsidian, 2 diamond gems and 1 book This is an expensive fortune pickaxe will pay for itself many times over when you start mining redstone, diamond and emerald.

It is recommended to have bookshelves around your enchantment table so you can get better enchantments for your items!

Minecraft Redstone Handbook
