REACTIVE OBJECTS

Redstone Devices

What can be Activated by Redstone?

These are objects that do something when they receive a redstone signal.

Doors and Gates

All doors and gates can be opened and closed using redstone. Iron doors can only be opened with redstone; wooden doors, fence gates and trapdoors can all be opened either by hand or with redstone. Why would you use an iron door? Wooden doors can be broken down by zombies if the game difficulty is set to hard, and villagers can open and close doors.

Redstone Lamps Crafting

Redstone lamps are made from redstone and glowstone, so you'll have to enter the Nether before you can make them. They light up when switched on by redestone electricity.


Pistons

Pistons are blocks that extend when activated by redstone power, giving the block next to them a push.
Normal Piston and Sticky Pistons
Normal pistons push a block, which then stays in its new position when the piston retracts. Addition of a slime ball creates a sticky piston, which pulls the block back when the piston retracts.
