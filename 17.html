Redstone Youtubers

Subscribing to Minecraft Youtubers

The Redstone Youtubers (Also known as Redstoners) are channels on Youtube that focus orimarily on redstone. Here are my faivourites:

Sethbling

Works mostly with command blocks, ussualy to create new concepts. Sethbling (who is also a MindCracker, or a member of the special MindCrack community of Youtubers) and has worked on a few bukkit plugins (for altering Bukkit servers) and MCEdit ( a program for editing worlds in minecraft) filters. He has created many minigames.

Mumbo Jumbo

Working primarily with redstone contraptions that are survival friendly, Mumbo Jumbo creates outstanding redstone tutorials, including the Batman equipment pole, a 2 X 2 hidden storage area and a variety of other contraptions.l He has made a few minigames and is a member of the Hermitcraft, another community or guild of Youtubers.
