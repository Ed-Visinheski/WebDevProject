Makes working models inMinecraft from real-life objects. Famous for his fully functional Minecraft printer, fully functional iPhone, fully functional escalator and his texture pack based on the Minecraft LEGO sets. ACtennisAC has made a couple of minigames including WII Sports, Angry Birds and Snowbording in Minecraft.

Minecraft Youtube Groups

You can find multiple Minecraft Youtube groups. The members of these groups are ussualy played on a multiplayer server that is whitelisted: Only player profiles listed on the whitelist are allowed to join. In these servers Redstoners help and prank each other, make videos and develop inventions.

Following Minecraft Youtube Groups

MindCrack

This official Youtube group is so poppular that the Mojang staff (the creators of Minecraft) are an unofficial part of the group. The MindCrack team even has their own booth to themselves at Minecon (Minecraft event).

Hermitcraft

A few members of this official Youtube group are related to MindCrack in some way. It only has 25 members and the server is currently not accepting applications for new members.
