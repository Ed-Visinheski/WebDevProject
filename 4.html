REDSTONE TORCH AND REPEATERS

WHAT IS IT FOR?

Redstone Torch

Tips:

You can craft a redstone torch from just a stick and a piace of redstone dust.
Redstone torches have a light level of 7 which is half of the light output in a regular torch. They wont melt ice or snow, and dont try using them as mob-spawning deterrent or you'll be in for nasty suprise!

A tick is an amount of time, and in redstone terms it refers to the time it takes the signal to travel from the power source to the object you are trying to power. A 1-tick delay is 0.1 seconds, a 2-tick delay is 0.2 and so on.

Redstone Repeaters

Did You Know?

By default, the repeater delay is set to 1 tick, but it can be changed to 2,3 or 4 ticks by hitting the use item button when the repeater is in place. You'll notice that the torch moves qalong each time you hit the repeater, signalling that you've changed the tick.

Redstone Torch

How it Works

You'll want to familiarise yourself with this handy item. A Redstone torch has several uses and, amongst other things, can act as a power source in a Redstone circuit.
Redstone torches are an essential ingredient in Redstone repeaters, Redstone comparators and activator rails.
Redstone torches have a 1 tick (0.1 second) delay when they are switched on or off. When used in log chains they can cause a few seconds' delay which you should factor in your calculations.

Redstone Repeaters

How a Repeater Works

A redstone repeater is a very useful item. Once you've wired it up the possibilities are endless!
A repeater will accept a signal from the back and push it out of the front with renewed signal strength. For reference, the back of the repeater is the side closest to you when you place it.

A repeater has several functions:

1:

To increase the distance a redsttone dust wire can send a signal. When added to a circuit, the repeater takes a signal of any strength and refreshes it so that it is repeated at full power (hence the name).

2:

To create a delay between receiving the signal and outputting the signal. This the most important function as the delay is critical to complicated machinery that requires specific timings to work correctly, such as a piston door. the maximum delqay that can be created by by a repeater is 0.4 seconds, but you can use several repeaters to create longer delays.
